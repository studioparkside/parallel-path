Landing Page Outline
Brand
Reciprocity
Parallel Path June 2026 Internal — Not for distribution

This is a content outline for the rebuilt Brand Reciprocity landing page — structure and tone only, not final copy. Eight sections from hero to CTA, followed by the full survey draft. Copy will be refined once we've aligned on direction and filled in the client examples.

Section 01
Hero
The opening statement

One or two lines. Not a tagline. A provocation. Something like: "Healthy living used to live in one place. Now it's everywhere. The brands that win aren't just louder. They're more trusted."

Below that, one sentence about Parallel Path — not credentials, a point of view.

Hero image or video background. Lifestyle photography. Real people, real places.
Section 02
The Shift
Why this moment is different

Two short paragraphs. The landscape has changed. Healthy living is no longer one category in one channel. The brands earning real trust are doing something different across three specific areas. Each one gets its own space on the page, not jammed together.

2–3 supporting stats from the existing consumer research. Each one given room to breathe, not stacked in a grid.
Section 03
The Framework
Identity. Connection. Experience.

The core graphic lives here. Introduced simply: a brand earns trust across three areas. When all three are working, consumers stop evaluating and start choosing.

Below the graphic, each area gets a short description and its building blocks. One section per area. Enough to understand it, not enough to overwhelm.

Core infographic — new visual direction TBD after mood board review.
Section 04
Is Your Brand Healthy?
The survey

Six questions. Multiple choice, all optional. No multi-page form. Submits inline, page doesn't refresh. Results delivered to email immediately.

Questions cover: which area the brand feels strongest in, where they feel the gap, whether they have existing partners for each area, and a couple of diagnostic questions about team and next steps.

The CTA is warm and specific: "Find out where your brand stands. Takes about two minutes."

Final six questions to be confirmed — see survey draft below.
Section 05
Our Work
Three examples from our clients

One per area. Real work, real results. The format is tight: brand category, the challenge, what we did, one outcome with a number. No brand name required if confidential.

Three client examples needed — one for Identity, one for Connection, one for Experience. One clear result or impact metric each.
Section 06
What Happens Next
A 30-minute conversation with our team

After you submit the survey, you'll hear from a real person — not a bot. Someone who has read your results and done a little research on your brand. If it makes sense, we'll offer a 30-minute call. We'll show you what we see and what we'd actually do about it.

Team photos, names, and LinkedIn links live here. The goal is to make clear that the people you're talking to are practitioners, not salespeople.

Team photos and LinkedIn profiles needed — John, Amanda, and at least one or two others.
Section 07
How We Work
This isn't a report. It's how we think.

Two short paragraphs. Brand Reciprocity isn't a one-time deliverable. It's the lens we use with every client. We ask different questions. We connect the dots between media strategy, brand identity, and customer experience because siloed conversations produce siloed results.

This section also signals what an ongoing relationship with Parallel Path looks like — without making it a pitch.

Section 08
Footer CTA
One ask. One button.

"Ready to see where your brand stands?"

Button: Take the survey.
Secondary: Schedule directly.
No other links competing for attention.
